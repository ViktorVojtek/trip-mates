# Trip Mates — Product Requirements Document

## Overview

Trip Mates is a social networking platform designed for couples, families, and individuals with busy lives who want to discover and connect with like-minded people to plan and embark on group trips. The platform matches users based on travel interests, availability, group composition preferences, and trip style — enabling people to build friendships through shared travel experiences without the friction of traditional networking.

## Problem

- Families and couples have limited free time due to work, childcare, and other responsibilities
- Traditional social venues (bars, clubs, community events) are often impractical or undesirable for many adults
- People want to travel with others but can't find groups that match their schedule, budget, family size, or travel style
- Existing travel platforms focus on logistics (booking, itineraries) rather than **social connection and group formation**

## Target Users

- **Busy couples** (25–55) looking to expand their social circle through travel
- **Families with children** who want other families to travel with for social and cost-sharing benefits
- **Solo adults or couples** who prefer group travel for safety, fun, and shared experiences
- **Travel enthusiasts** of all ages who want to build lasting friendships through adventure

## Core Features

1. **Profile Creation** — Users create detailed profiles including family size, ages of children, travel preferences, availability, and personality indicators
2. **Trip Discovery Feed** — Browse trips posted by other users or groups, filtered by date, location, group type, and travel style
3. **Trip Posting** — Users can propose trips with details: destination, dates, budget, group composition needed, and activity preferences
4. **Smart Matching** — Algorithm matches users based on compatibility of schedules, interests, family composition, travel budget, and trip style
5. **Group Chat** — In-app messaging for trip planning discussions among matched users or group members
6. **Availability Calendar** — Visual calendar showing when users are free, helping coordinate schedules
7. **Trust & Safety** — Profile verification, reporting system, and community guidelines to ensure a safe environment

## Non-Goals

- Travel booking and payment processing (integrations may exist, but we don't handle transactions)
- AI-generated itineraries or travel planning assistance
- Hotel, flight, or accommodation booking
- Real-time trip tracking or navigation features
- Global translation services or multi-language UI in v1
- Advanced video calling or voice chat features

## Success Metrics

- **User Acquisition**: 1,000 active users within the first 3 months
- **Engagement**: Average session duration > 5 minutes; 3+ profile interactions per user per week
- **Trips Booked**: 200+ trip connections facilitated within the first 3 months
- **Retention**: 40% of users return within 7 days; 25% return within 30 days
- **User Satisfaction**: Net Promoter Score (NPS) > 50 based on post-trip surveys
- **Matching Rate**: >60% of trip posts result in at least one interested match within 7 days
